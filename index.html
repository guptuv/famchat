History

The group's
journey.

June 2021 · Group begins
A quiet start 👋
218 messages in the first month. Mummy kicks things off with a viral Hindi milk-roti recipe joke. Papa BSNL is the early dominant voice with an 8 AM peak — the group's only genuine early bird.
September 5, 2021 · All-time record day
🎂 Revant's birthday blast — 453 messages
The group's single-day record, 10× the daily average. Papa BSNL sends 20+ media files consecutively. Every member shows up. The family's greatest collective effort in one day.
Oct – Dec 2021
The quiet phase
Activity drops to 46–112 messages per month. The group rests. Only 254 total messages across 3 months — a stark contrast to the birthday chaos.
November 28, 2021
Papa Jio joins the chat
He enters quietly but transforms the group. Over 5 years he becomes the #1 conversation starter with 1,181 thread initiations — more than Yuvraj and Puja di combined.
2022 · Education season
📚 JEE & CBSE exam discussions
205 exam-related messages concentrated in 2022. JEE Main dates, CBSE result links, admit card updates — the group becomes a family information relay board.
November 2022 · Monthly peak ever
🔥 1,276 messages in one month
The group's busiest month until 2026. All members simultaneously active. High-energy family communication at its most intense.
December 25, 2022
🎄 Christmas — 122 messages
Second busiest single day. Yuvraj opens with "Merry Christmas Dhunmun 🥳🥳🥳" and the family joins in. A warm, celebratory day that shows the group at its most joyful.
July 22, 2023
Papa BSNL goes silent forever
Last message July 22, 2023. From 829 messages in 2021 to 12 in 2023 to nothing. His BSNL number was likely replaced by Papa Jio's Jio SIM — a quiet generational phone upgrade.
2024 – 2025 · Maturity
Yuvraj becomes the heartbeat
3,136 messages in 2025 alone. Papa Jio also surges to 2,153. The group averages 20+ messages per day — a true daily communication hub for the whole family.
April – May 2026 · Right now
🔥 New records — the group is growing
April 2026 hit 1,082 messages — the new monthly record. May 2026 is at 1,022. After 5 years, this family is more connected than ever. The story isn't over.

Narrative

The story
of the group.

This group began in June 2021 with Mummy sharing a classic Hindi joke about a milk-roti recipe going viral on Facebook. It was small — 218 messages that month — but the warmth was already there. Papa BSNL was the loudest voice in those early days, dominating September 2021 with a historic birthday media dump for Revant: 453 messages in a single day, the group's all-time record for years.

Then something shifted. Papa Jio joined in November 2021 and slowly became the group's anchor — its most consistent conversation-starter with 1,181 thread initiations over 5 years. He doesn't always say the most, but he's the one who opens the door every morning, drops a YouTube Short, and gets everyone talking. He is the reason this group rarely goes silent.

Yuvraj grew into the dominant voice: from 178 messages in 2021 to 3,136 in 2025. He is the heartbeat of the group now — thoughtful texts, late-night conversations at 10 PM, the one who always responds. In 2026, he's already sent 1,552 messages and shows no sign of slowing.

Puja di operates differently from everyone else. She sends the fewest words per message (2.7 average) but the most media files (4,975 total). She is the group's silent broadcaster — images, reels, and emoji reactions over long text. Her ❤️ and 😂 do more emotional work than most paragraphs could.

Papa BSNL quietly faded after mid-2023. From 829 messages in 2021, to 12 in 2023, to silence. His BSNL number was likely replaced by the Papa Jio SIM. The group never skipped a beat. And Mummy holds it all together emotionally — her 😘 and 😊 and 🤣 are a constant presence across every phase, every year, every milestone this chat has witnessed.

Today in 2026, with over 30,000 messages across 5 years, this is a family that has chosen to stay close — not just in person, but daily, digitally, in a WhatsApp group that gets louder every year. A digital living room. Always open. Never quiet for long.
