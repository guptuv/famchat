History

The group's
journey.

June 2021 · Group begins
A quiet start 👋
218 messages in the first month. Mummy kicks things off with a viral Hindi milk-roti recipe joke. Papa BSNL is the early dominant voice.
September 5, 2021 · All-time record
🎂 Revant's birthday blast — 453 messages in one day
The group's single-day record, 10× the daily average. Papa BSNL sends 20+ media files consecutively in one sitting. The family brought everything they had.
Oct – Dec 2021 · Quiet phase
Silence settles in
Activity drops to just 46–112 messages per month. Likely a period of routine offline family life. The group rests.
November 2021 · New voice
Papa Jio joins the chat
First message on Nov 28, 2021. He enters quietly but steadily becomes the group's top conversation-starter — 1,181 thread initiations over 5 years.
2022 · Education season
📚 JEE, CBSE exam discussions peak
205 exam-related messages across the dataset, concentrated heavily in 2022. JEE Main updates, CBSE term result links — the group becomes an information relay.
November 2022 · Monthly peak
🔥 1,276 messages — the busiest month ever
All members are highly active simultaneously. A period of rich family engagement that hasn't been replicated until 2026.
December 25, 2022
🎄 Christmas — 122 messages
Second busiest single day. Yuvraj opens with "Merry Christmas Dhunmun" and the whole family joins in. Warm, celebratory, festive.
July 2023 · An era ends
Papa BSNL goes silent
Last message July 22, 2023. From 829 messages in 2021 to 12 in 2023 to nothing. His BSNL number was likely replaced by Papa Jio's — a generational phone upgrade.
2024 – 2025 · Maturity
Yuvraj becomes the dominant voice
3,136 messages in 2025 alone — more than double his 2021 count. Papa Jio also surges to 2,153. The group is now a true daily communication hub.
April – May 2026 · Right now
🔥 Most active period ever — and growing
April 2026 hit 1,082 messages. May is on track at 1,022. After 5 years, this family group shows no signs of slowing — it's more alive than the day it started.

Narrative

The story
of the group.

This group began in June 2021 with Mummy sharing a classic Hindi joke about a milk-roti recipe going viral on Facebook. It was small — 218 messages that month — but the warmth was already there. Papa BSNL was the loudest voice in those early days, dominating September 2021 with a historic birthday media dump for Revant: 453 messages in a single day, the group's all-time record, and a number that stood unchallenged for years.

Then something shifted. Papa Jio joined in November 2021 and slowly became the group's anchor — its most consistent conversation-starter with 1,181 thread initiations over 5 years. He doesn't always say the most, but he's the one who opens the door every morning, drops a YouTube Short, and gets everyone talking.

Yuvraj grew into the dominant voice: from 178 messages in 2021 to 3,136 in 2025. He's the heartbeat of the group now — thoughtful texts, late-night conversations, the one who always responds. His peak is 10 PM, long after everyone else has quieted down.

Puja di operates differently from everyone else. She sends the fewest words per message but the most media files. She is the group's silent broadcaster — images, reels, and reactions over long text. Her ❤️ and 😂 do more work than most paragraphs.

Papa BSNL quietly faded after mid-2023 — his BSNL number likely replaced by Papa Jio's Jio SIM. The group never skipped a beat. And Mummy holds it all together emotionally: her 😘 and 😊 and 🤣 are a constant presence across every phase, every year, every milestone the chat has witnessed.

Today in 2026, with over 30,000 messages across 5 years, this family chat is genuinely one of the most consistent and growing WhatsApp groups you'll find. A digital living room that gets louder every year — and shows no signs of stopping.
